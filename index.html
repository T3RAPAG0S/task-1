Introduction

Rowlet is a dual-type Grass/Flying Pokémon introduced in Generation VII. It is the starter Pokémon of the Alola region and evolves into Dartrix and eventually Decidueye. Rowlet has a round, owl-like appearance with a leaf bowtie, making it one of the most adorable Pokémon in the series!

Shiny Rowlet - The Unique Grass Quill Pokémon

Shiny Rowlet is a rare alternate color variant of the adorable Grass/Flying-type Pokémon Rowlet. Unlike the regular Rowlet, Shiny Rowlet features a striking color palette, with its feathers changing from the typical green and brown to a much lighter, almost golden shade, making it a fan-favorite for collectors and enthusiasts. The Shiny form of Rowlet is sought after for its rarity, as it has a very low chance of appearing in the wild or being bred in Pokémon games. Despite this color variation, Shiny Rowlet retains all the charm and characteristics of its original form. It still possesses its signature leaf bowtie, round owl-like eyes, and the calm demeanor that makes Rowlet one of the most beloved starter Pokémon from the Alola region. If you are lucky enough to encounter one, Shiny Rowlet is a truly beautiful and unique addition to any Pokémon team!
